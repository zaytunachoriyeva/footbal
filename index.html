Barcelona Football Club (Football Club Barcelona) is a team from the city of Barcelona. Representative of the Spanish Premiere Division. Founded in 1899 by Swiss, British, Spanish and Catalan players under the leadership of Joan Gamper. The club has become one of the symbols of Catalonia, which is why it is called El Barça es mes que un club ("Barça" is more than a club). Barcelona fans are known as "culés". In 2006, the number of members exceeded 140,000.
